To set up vaccination reminders:

  1. Go to your pet's profile page
  2. Tap on "Health Records"
  3. Select "Vaccinations"
  4. Tap "Add Reminder" next to the vaccination
  5. Set the date and notification preferences
  6. Tap "Save"

You'll receive notifications based on your settings before the vaccination is due.

To cancel or reschedule an appointment:

  1. Go to the "Appointments" section in the app
  2. Find and tap on the appointment you want to change
  3. Select "Reschedule" or "Cancel" at the bottom of the screen
  4. If rescheduling, choose a new date and time
  5. Confirm your changes

Note: Some clinics may have cancellation policies. Check the appointment details for any fees that might apply.

To add multiple pets to your account:

  1. Go to the "Pets" tab in the main menu
  2. Tap the "+" button or "Add Pet" option
  3. Fill in your pet's details (name, species, breed, age, etc.)
  4. Upload a photo if you'd like
  5. Tap "Save" to add the pet to your profile

You can add up to 10 pets on a standard account. Premium accounts have unlimited pet profiles.

To change your subscription plan:

  1. Go to "Account" in the main menu
  2. Select "Subscription & Billing"
  3. Tap "Change Plan"
  4. Choose your new plan from the options
  5. Review the changes and billing information
  6. Confirm your selection

Plan changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Upgrading mid-cycle may result in a prorated charge.

To post in the community forum:

  1. Go to the "Community" tab
  2. Tap the "+" button or "Start New Discussion" at the bottom of the screen
  3. Select a category for your post
  4. Add a title and write your message
  5. Add any photos if needed
  6. Tap "Post" to publish

Please review our community guidelines before posting. All new members have a brief moderation period for their first 3 posts.

To reset your password, tap on "Forgot Password" on the login screen. Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your email. For security reasons, password reset links expire after 24 hours.

Go to "Account" in the main menu, then select "Personal Information." Here you can update your name, email, phone number, and address. Remember to tap "Save" after making changes.
